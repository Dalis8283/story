“Thank you Lakeman, but as we have the evacuees here, it will be a little different from now on. Any late boy will be required to return at 1pm for his punishment, and then stand in the corner, hands on head, for the whole afternoon.”

He gave us an insincere smile.

“Have a nice swim. See you all tomorrow.”
