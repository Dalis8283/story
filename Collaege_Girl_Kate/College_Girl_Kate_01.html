Introduction
 
"Kate” is Katherine Rose Brady, 18 years old, from Long Island, New York. There is an exchange program with nearby Bridger Military Academy a mostly male school some 20 miles away. A few male cadets attend McClintok morning classes and a few upper-level girls from McClintok take science labs in the afternoons at Bridger.
 
McClintok has a school uniform for girls, which they wear at all times on campus except to gym classes. It consists of: black and gold skirt, white blouse, gold regulation panties, black blazer with school logo on left breast, black and gold diagonal stripped tie, gold knee socks, white and black Mary Janes. PJ’s are worn with rubber-soled slippers. The Gym uniform is a light shade of green-gold with a loose-fitting blouse and bloomers. Worn with white athletic socks and black and gold tennis shoes.
 
All girls who enter McClintok must sign a waiver in which they agree to accept "reasonable" corporal punishment for their misdeeds. Parents and/or guardians also sign the waiver. Boys from Bridger are not spanked at McClintock and girls from McClintock are not spanked at Bridger. If a student gets into trouble at the ‘other” school, he or she is reported to the appropriate “home” school. School faculty and staff at McClintock are permitted to spank students on the panties or bare bottom with their hands or school-approved instruments.
